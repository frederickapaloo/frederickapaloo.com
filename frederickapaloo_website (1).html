"What if Eden was never just a location — but a condition of the heart?"

In The Eden Within, Frederick Apaloo takes readers on a profound journey through three gardens — Eden, Gethsemane, and the garden of the heart — where every person must decide daily between their own desires and the will of God.

This book is for the believer struggling in secret, the young person overwhelmed by desires they cannot control, and anyone who hungers for deeper intimacy with God.

About

Called to Guard
the Inner Man

Frederick Edem Apaloo is a reverend minister, biblical teacher, and author dedicated to raising believers who are spiritually grounded and inwardly transformed. With a passion for Scripture and heart-centred discipleship, he teaches on purity, desire, relationships, and practical Christian living.

Through preaching, mentoring, and writing, Frederick calls this generation back to the one thing that matters most — the condition of the heart. He believes that when the inner man is right with God, every other area of life follows.

His ministry spans the pulpit, the printed page, and personal discipleship — always with one goal: helping people cultivate a heart that is fully surrendered to God.

✦ Ministry Affiliation Frederick serves as a pastor at Victory Bible Church International, Ghana. His personal ministry, however, extends far beyond any single local church — reaching believers across Ghana and globally through writing, speaking, and digital outreach.
